Cancellation Policy

A weekly lesson time is reserved for your student and regular attendance is necessary for steady progress. If you need to cancel a lesson, you must notify me at least 24 hours in advance. This will guarantee a make-up or credit for the lesson. If I receive less than 24 hours notice, you will be required to pay for the lesson. If I am unable to teach a scheduled lesson, you will be given a credit or refund for the lesson.
